About Us

Our history and Our Beliefs

We solve your Material supply chain issues!

Ours is a team that strives to solve all your chemical supply needs in the fastest time.
The rapid development in the domain of chemistry has ushered in an altogether new era of industrialization, driven to a large extent by the myriad advantages offered by innovative and more efficient chemical substitutes of the erstwhile natural extracts. Take for example the case of dyes used in the textile industry, once dependent upon natural sources for colored pigments which neither lasted long nor their color palette was as broad, and now have been substituted by synthetic dyes, which are easier to work with and offer greater versatility in terms of hues.
Since its inception in 1991, R. H. Dye Chem has been a revered name in the domain of industrial utility chemicals, in particular the ones brought to use in textile and perfumery industries, on account of its excellent gamut of products which are trusted for their high efficiency, reactivity, and reliability. Backed by a team of experts, we have established ourselves as an eminent Manufacturer, Supplier, Exporter, and Trader of chemical paraphernalia that belong to the category of Perfume Chemicals, chemicals for the Paint Industry, Textiles Chemicals, Textile Processing Chemicals, Textile Industry Chemicals, Chemicals for Ink Industry, Laundry Chemicals, Chemicals for Leather Industry, etc.
